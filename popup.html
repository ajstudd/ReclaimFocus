ReclaimFocus

Build a system that keeps you focused, disciplined, and in control of your time.

ReclaimFocus exists to help you reach your peak potential. The web rewards attention with novelty. This extension is a quiet system that returns attention to you: block what pulls you off course, cap the time you give to distractions, and schedule what matters so it actually happens.

Discipline is not punishment. It is the design of an environment where doing the right thing is the easiest thing. Every feature here exists for that reason: blocked sites that redirect to somewhere meaningful, time-limited browsing with honest cooldowns, keyword filters, and task reminders that nudge you toward your day.

Your data stays yours. Everything ReclaimFocus tracks lives only in your browser. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no third parties. Export your config, import it, reset it, or wipe it. The controls are entirely in your hands.
